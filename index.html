Change Your Habits

Old habits die hard. Changing your habits is a process that involves several stages. Sometimes it takes a while before changes become new habits. And, you may face roadblocks along the way. Movement, Time, Practice, and Weight Loss are all great starting points. Here are some ways:

Movement

Aim for 30 minutes of daily movement, find your fit, and every step counts.

Time

Prioritize what matters, schedule self-care, and make time for healthy habits.

Practice

Celebrate small wins, stick with your routine for lasting change, and adjust your practice as needed.

Weight Loss

Focus on healthy eating, Find a sustainable calorie deficit, and Combine healthy eating with exercise.

BMI Calculator

A BMI (Body Mass Index) Calculator measures body fat based on height and weight. It helps determine if a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese, providing a quick assessment of health status. This tool is widely used in healthcare to guide weight management and health improvement strategies.
